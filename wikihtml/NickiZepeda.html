I am currently a soon to be graduate of Theatre Arts and Foreign Language major with a radio, television, film minor. I aspire to work on international films with the goal of connecting and uniting people, their heritage and ethnicity in conjunction with the arts. While dilegently pursueing the american dream with as minimal beaurotic influence as possible, I await and search for projects that reflect and question the human spirit rather than a person's bank account.
