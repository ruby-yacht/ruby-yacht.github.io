round 2:

player 3 claims to have one 6
round 3:
player 1 has one seven
player two claims to have one 8
player 3 claims to have one 9
round 4:
player 1 has one ten
player 2 claims to have one jack
player 3 claims to have 2 queens
round 5:
player 1 has one king and resets the counter
player 2 claims to have one ace
player three claims to have two twos and is called by player 1, they were twos and player 1 picks up all 16 cards

the game continues from here with player 1 being able to play large amounts of cards, and no one calling out that they doubt it. There are a few skipped rounds ending with player 3 playing the ace of diamonds.
