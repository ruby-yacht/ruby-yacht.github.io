we are playing like designers (Extra Credits)

  1. start the lab quiz, you will have 24 hours to complete it
  2. play games in teams of 2 or 3 http://ruby-yacht.github.io/games/
  3. record your partner(s) in the quiz
  4. take notes, watch each other play (share your screen during play, each person should share one game)
  5. spending 5-10 minutes on each game, teams to not need to play the same games, but you should observe your partner and play at least one game
  6. make one critical observation for each game (you may discuss this with your partner(s))
  7. GAME #1 play This is the Only Level (http://armorgames.com/play/4309/this-is-the-only-level this was flash)
  8. GAME #2 play a game from a previous semester
  9. GAME #3 play a game from a former exam
  10. GAME #4 play 1 more game from this list
  11. GAME #5 play a game that you and your partner agree on (both play) or another game from this list
  12. GAME #6 play a dating sim https://itch.io/games/tag-dating-sim or https://itch.io/games/tag-dating-sim/tag-weird (find an unusual one)
  13. find an element in each of the 6 games that interest you, prepare to make a critical comment
  14. Briefly mention a game that you watched being played and played yourself. How was the experience different (this will be part of your next blog).
  15. finish the lab quiz today based on your notes
